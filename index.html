ABOUT US

Meet the Bhookies

At Bhookie, we’re all about bold flavors, big cravings, and unforgettable bites. Born from a passion for street-style fusion food, Bhookie brings together the best of global comfort dishes with a desi twist. Whether it’s our loaded burgers, spicy wraps, crispy fries, or signature street bites — every item is crafted to deliver a punch of flavor and satisfaction in every bite.

About

Bhookie is a bold and fresh quick-bite fusion food startup bringing the taste of India to the streets of Liverpool, UK. With a unique focus on iconic items like Vada Pav and Chicken Fries, we’re redefining street food culture abroad. Bhookie blends tradition and creativity — delivering memorable bites packed with flavor and personality.
